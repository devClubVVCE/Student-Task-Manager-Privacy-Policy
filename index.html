PRIVACY POLICY

Introduction

Student Task Manager(STM) is a project developed by the students of Developers Club, Vidhyavardhaka College of Engineering. STM is an easy-to-use and great tool to manage all your task in a single place. It helps students to plan, organize, prioritize and make the most out of their time.

The app does not collect any user information.

Log data

STM collects data related to app crashes and performance with the sole purpose of improving the app's usability

Selling Ads

The App does not sell Ads currently, therefore you will not find third-party links to redirect you somewhere else. Subject to change later but will be updated.
